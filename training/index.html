Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers to some common queries about our training programs.

Do I need any prior experience to join?

Our "Solar Installation Fundamentals" course is designed for complete beginners with no prior experience. For our advanced courses, some foundational knowledge or experience in electrical work is recommended.

What is the duration of the courses?

Course durations vary. The Fundamentals course is typically a 2-week intensive program, while advanced courses can range from 3 to 4 weeks. We also offer flexible weekend options.

Will I receive a certificate after the training?

Yes, upon successful completion of any of our courses, you will receive an industry-recognized certificate from Kompetent Solar Academy, which will boost your credibility and employability.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

While we do not guarantee job placement, we offer career support services including CV reviews, interview preparation, and connecting our top graduates with our network of partners in the solar industry.
